Fruity Pill

1. Introduction

The name of my product is "Fruity Pill". It is a healthy fruit snack.

2. Product Description

Fruity Pill is a small, colorful pill. It comes in many bright colors, and tastes like real fruit.

It is made by mixing fruit flavors and healthy ingredients together. Then the mixture is made into a pill shape. The ingredients are grapes, pineapples, oranges, apples, pears, bananas, and ice cream flavor.

It is healthy because it contains fruit and natural ingredients.

The available flavors are grape, pineapple, orange, apple, pear, and banana.

3. Persuasion

People should buy Fruity Pill because it is delicious, healthy, and fun to eat.

It tastes sweet, fruity, smooth, and creamy. People feel happy and excited when they eat Fruity Pill.

4. Conclusion

People can buy Fruity Pill at supermarkets, convenience stores, and online. One Fruity Pill pack costs $2.99.

Slogan: "Fruity Pill – All Your Favorite Fruits in One Delicious Pill!"~

Food Product Writing Prompts

1. Introduction
•.What is the name of your product?
•.What type of food or drink is it?

2. Product Description
•.What does it look like?
•.How is it made?
•.What ingredients are used?
•.Is it healthy? Why or why not?
•.What flavors are available?

3. Persuasion
•.Why should people buy your product?
•.How does it taste?
•.How do people feel when they eat or drink it?

4. Conclusion
•.Where can people buy it?
•.How much does it cost?
•.What is your slogan or jingle?~
